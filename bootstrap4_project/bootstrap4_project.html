How long are the hikes up the volcanoes?

Mt. Lassen takes 3-5 hours to complete, Mt. Hood takes 6-9 hours, Mt. Shasta takes around 9 hours.

Mt. Lassen has a vertical gain of around 2,000 feet, making it great for beginners. Mt. Hood is a bit harder at around 4,300 feet for our specific trail. Mt. Shasta is the hardest of our hikes with an elevation of around 7,300 feet.
